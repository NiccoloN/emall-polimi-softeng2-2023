title End User cancels a Booking

actor End User
participant eMSS
participant CPMS


End User->eMSS:get(personalPage)
activate eMSS
eMSS->eMSS:load(bookingList, chargeHistory)
eMSS-->End User:show(personalPage)
deactivate eMSS


End User->eMSS:cancel(booking)
activate eMSS
eMSS->CPMS:cancel(booking)
activate CPMS
CPMS->CPMS:search(booking)
CPMS->CPMS:delete(booking)
CPMS-->eMSS:response
deactivate CPMS
eMSS-->End User:show(response)
deactivate eMSS
